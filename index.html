By paulo          march 03 2019

sweeping shots of the iconic christ the reedemer monument
overlooking rio de janerio. beyond its breathtaking views the
statue has a colourful history thats worth exploring, and a future
that could leave it looking very different from how it does today.

  1. IT CAME IN RESPONSE TO"AN ADVANCING TIDE OF GODLESNESS".

    2. seperated church an state, Fear began to grow amongst catholics
